Profile

Hello! I'm a passionate and innovative digital creator based in Youngsville, North Carolina. Currently, I'm pursuing my Graphic Design Degree at Wake Technical College, where I am honing my skills in Graphic Design, Web Design, Digital Illustration, Photography, Content Creation, and Social Media Marketing.

My journey in digital creation began at a young age sitting at my parents' computer creating works of art on the old Paint program, exploring font types, and organizing digital photographs, fueled by a fascination with creating unique designs on a digital medium. Over the years, I've developed a diverse portfolio that includes logo designs, digital illustrations, custom digital creations printed on gifts, and professional quality photography.
